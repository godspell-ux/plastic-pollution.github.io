  1. Use reusable shopping bags
  2. Carry a reusable water bottle

  4. Use beeswax wraps instead of cling film
  5. Buy in bulk to reduce packaging
  6. Choose bar soap over liquid in plastic bottles
  7. Use a bamboo toothbrush
  8. Bring your own coffee cup
  9. Say no to plastic straws
  10. Use reusable produce bags
